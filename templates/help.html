Who We Are:

About The Team

We are a group of four software engineering students in our final year of university, passionate about building innovative solutions. Our latest project is a hospital wait time prediction service that aims to improve the patient experience.

We recognized that wait times are not only frustrating for patients but can also have serious consequences for their health. We decided to leverage our software engineering skills to create a solution that could help hospitals better manage their patient flow and reduce wait times.

Our Goal

The goal of our predictive model is to help hospital emergency departments allocate resources to ensure prepardness when the time is right.

Our team has experience in developing machine learning models and data analysis tools. We knew that building an accurate prediction service would require gathering and analyzing data from multiple sources. We collected and used this data to develop machine learning models that predict wait times based on various factors.

We are excited about the potential impact of our project and believe that it could make a real difference in the healthcare industry.
